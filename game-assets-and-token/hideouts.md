# 🌲 Hideouts

![Tier 1 — Forest Hollow](../.gitbook/assets/hideout_forest_hollow.jpg)

Your hideout is your base of operations. It determines how many Merry Men you can deploy and how much total upkeep you can sustain. It is your ceiling — and upgrading it is the primary driver of growth in HOODLOOT.

---

## Key Attributes

Each hideout tier has three stats that define what you can do inside it:

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **$SHARE Cost** | The $SHARE required to upgrade. 75% of this is given to the poor permanently. |
| **Slots** | The maximum number of Merry Men you can place simultaneously. |
| **Upkeep Cap (U)** | The maximum total upkeep of all placed outlaws combined. |

{% hint style="danger" %}
**The total upkeep of all outlaws in your hideout cannot exceed the upkeep cap.** If you want to deploy more powerful Merry Men, upgrade your hideout first — or remove lower-efficiency outlaws to free up upkeep.
{% endhint %}

---

## 🪙 ETH Tolls

Every other hideout tier requires an ETH payment alongside the $SHARE cost. These are **one-time milestone payments** — not recurring fees — valued in USD via the Chainlink ETH/USD oracle.

| Tier | Hideout | ETH Required |
|:----:|---------|:------------:|
| T1 | Forest Hollow | ~$30 USD |
| T3 | Greenwood Camp | ~$50 USD |
| T5 | Sherwood Stronghold | ~$70 USD |
| T7 | Nottingham Safehouse | ~$100 USD |
| T9 | Sherwood Throne | ~$150 USD |

ETH tolls go directly to the HOODLOOT War Chest treasury — the protocol's hard gatekeeping mechanism ensuring progression milestones carry real weight.

---

## ⏱️ Upgrade Cooldown

{% hint style="warning" %}
There is a **24-hour cooldown** (864,000 blocks) between each hideout upgrade. You cannot rush through multiple tiers in a single session. Plan your upgrades accordingly.
{% endhint %}

## 📐 Strategy Notes

{% hint style="info" %}
**T5 → T6:** Slot count stays at 20, but upkeep nearly doubles (7,000 U → 13,125 U). This is an upkeep efficiency upgrade — if you have 20 slots filled with mid-tier outlaws, T6 lets you swap to far more powerful Merry Men without waiting for T7.

**T7 — Nottingham Safehouse** is the current live ceiling: 24 slots and 20,000 U. **The Undercroft (T8)** and the **Sherwood Throne (T9)** are coming soon — end-game Loot Rate stacks arrive with them.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="success" %}
**Which outlaws fit where.** Your Hideout's upkeep cap is the real constraint — slots run out slower than upkeep does. Match your Hideout tier to the [Merry Men](outlaws.md) you intend to run: a Forest Hollow (28 U) can just about hold four early outlaws; a Nottingham Safehouse (20,000 U) fields a serious elven band across its 24 slots. Upgrade the ceiling, then fill it.
{% endhint %}
